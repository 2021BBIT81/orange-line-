About Us

Step into a new era of modern transportation with the Lahore Orange Line Metro Train. Our system is not just a mode of transit; it's a symbol of cutting-edge connectivity and efficiency right in the heart of Lahore. Launched in 2020, the Orange Line has swiftly and decisively redefined the city's approach to urban transit.

Connectivity into future by enabling Belt & Road Initiative and a reliable partner in Urban Development.
